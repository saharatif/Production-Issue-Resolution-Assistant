Manufacturing: Production Issue Resolution Assistant
A manufacturing operations manager oversees several plants or production lines and needs quick insight
when something goes wrong — quality issues, throughput drops, supplier delays, equipment downtime,
or safety concerns.
The solution accepts:
A short problem statement (e.g., “output dropped on Line B,” “defect spike,” “missed supplier delivery”),
plant or line ID, timeframe, and any optional logs or notes.
The solution produces:
 A rootcause hypothesis with confidence levels
 Actionable recommendations broken into “Stabilize / Investigate / Prevent recurrence”
 A short explanation referencing process standards, prior incidents, maintenance history, or quality
thresholds
 A nextstep package (e.g., draft a shift handoff note, create a maintenance request, outline a
corrective action plan, generate questions for suppliers)
